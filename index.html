Person biography

Laigang Guo received his Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from UCAS in 2019. He was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Institute of Network Coding at CUHK and in the National Center for Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences at CAS, respectively, from 2019 to 2021. Since Sept. 2021, he has been an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Beijing Normal University. His research interests include symbolic computation, information theory, and dynamical systems. He is a recipient of the President Award of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
